About Our Ecommerce Platform

A modern online marketplace where users can browse, buy, and sell products easily and securely.

Who We Are

Our Ecommerce Web Application was developed as part of a CSCI 313 project. The goal of this platform is to simulate a real-world online marketplace where customers can browse products, manage their shopping cart, and complete a secure checkout process.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a simple, user friendly, and responsive online shopping experience. The system is designed with clean navigation, secure authentication, and efficient product management features.

What We Offer

  • User account registration and login
  • Product browsing and search functionality
  • Shopping cart system
  • Simulated checkout process
  • Order history tracking
  • Admin product management
